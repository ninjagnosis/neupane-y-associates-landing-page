## Mark Your Calendar

The Nepali fiscal year (Baishakh 1 – Chaitra 31) brings a cascade of tax deadlines. Missing even one can trigger penalties, assessments, and audit red flags. Here's what you need to know for FY 2082/83.

### Monthly Obligations

**TDS Withholding & Depositing**
- Businesses are required to withhold TDS on payments to contractors, consultants, and vendors.
- Deposit deadlines: 15th of every month (for withholdings from the prior month)
- Missing the deadline? Late-payment interest of 1% per month applies immediately.

**VAT Filing & Depositing**
- VAT returns are filed monthly via the IRD portal.
- Due date: 15th of the following month.
- Even if VAT is nil, the return must be filed.

### Quarterly Checkpoints

**Q1 (Baishakh – Ashadh)**
- Employee Provident Fund (EPF): Deposit contributions by the last day of Ashadh.
- Workmen's Compensation: File quarterly reports with the Social Welfare Council.

**Q2 & Q3 (Shrawan – Kartik & Mansir – Phalgun)**
- Repeat TDS, VAT, and EPF obligations.
- Monitor income threshold if you're approaching tax-bracket changes.

### Year-End Critical Dates

**Chaitra 29 – Financial Year Closing**
- Finalize all ledgers and accounts.
- Reconcile bank accounts.
- Record any year-end adjustments.

**Magh 15 (Annual)**
- Annual financial statements must be ready for audit preparation.

**Falgun 15 – Audit Submission Window Opens**
- Audited financial statements must be submitted to the Office of the Company Registrar (OCR).
- Failing to file audited accounts can result in penalties and director disqualifications.

**Chaitra 31 – FY Close Deadline**
- All prior-year filings (company, tax, audit) must be submitted.

### Pro Tips to Stay Compliant

1. **Automate TDS Tracking**: Use accounting software to calculate and track TDS automatically. Manual tracking invites errors.
2. **VAT on Imports**: Remember that VAT applies to both goods and services imported from outside Nepal. Keep customs documentation.
3. **Amended Returns**: If you filed a return with errors, don't panic. Amended returns can be filed within the same fiscal year.
4. **Documentation**: Keep invoices, receipts, and payment proof for a minimum of 6 years. IRD audits often reach back that far.

### What Happens If You Miss a Deadline?

- **TDS Late Deposit**: 1% interest per month, plus potential notice from IRD
- **VAT Late Filing**: 10% of VAT liability (minimum NRs 500) plus interest
- **Company Filing Late**: Director penalties up to NRs 10,000; potential public notice
- **Audit Report Late**: Company can be delisted from the OCR registry

### Common Mistakes We See

- Filing VAT without matching invoices (causes interest assessments)
- Withholding TDS but forgetting to deposit (triggers automatic penalty)
- Year-end provisions recorded without proper documentation
- Misclassifying contractor payments (should have TDS applied)
