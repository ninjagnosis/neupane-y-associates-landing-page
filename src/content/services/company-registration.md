## From Idea to Incorporated

Starting a business involves multiple registrations, approvals, and compliance obligations. We handle the paperwork so you can focus on building. From initial company registration through tax enrollment, we ensure your new entity is properly established and ready to operate.

### Registration Process

1. **Incorporation**: OCR registration with approved MOA and AOA
2. **Tax Registration**: PAN assignment and VAT enrollment (if applicable)
3. **Employment Setup**: Social Security, Workmen's Compensation, and Health Insurance
4. **Banking & Statutory**: Bank account opening and initial compliance filings
5. **Ongoing Support**: First-year compliance and filing assistance

### Why Choose Us

- **End-to-End Service**: One point of contact for all registrations.
- **Fast Turnaround**: Relationships with OCR and tax authority accelerate approvals.
- **Compliance-Ready**: Your company emerges ready to file, not scrambling to catch up.
