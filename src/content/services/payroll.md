## Payroll That Complies

Payroll is complex: salary calculations, TDS withholding, provident fund contributions, and statutory registrations. We manage all of it, ensuring employees are paid correctly and on time while you stay compliant with Nepal's employment and tax laws.

### Payroll Services

- **Salary Processing**: Calculation, deductions, advances, and final settlement
- **Statutory Deductions**: TDS withholding and quarterly IRD filing
- **Employee Benefits**: EPF contribution management and member statements
- **Pay Slips & Certificates**: Monthly pay slips and annual tax certificates (Form 15)
- **Record Keeping**: Employee register, attendance tracking, and compliance documentation
- **Compliance**: Employment letter approvals, policy documentation, and statutory filings

### Why Choose Us

- **Accuracy**: Automated calculations reduce manual error.
- **Compliance**: All statutory requirements met and documented.
- **Transparency**: Employees get clear pay slips; you get transparent records.
