## Regulatory-Grade Confidence

Every number matters in an audit. We conduct independent financial statement audits to Nepal Auditing Standards (NAS), ensuring your financial reports are reliable, accurate, and audit-ready. Whether you're a growing company seeking investor confidence or an NGO filing annual returns to the Social Welfare Council, we deliver audits that stand up to scrutiny.

### Our Process

We begin with a thorough understanding of your business, industry risks, and control environment. We then test transactions, verify balances with third parties, and assess the overall reasonableness of your financial position. The result is a clean or qualified audit opinion and a management letter highlighting opportunities to strengthen controls.

### Why Choose Us

- **Nepal-Specific Expertise**: We understand OCR filing requirements, tax audit implications, and sector-specific regulations.
- **Timely Delivery**: Audits completed on your schedule, with interim reporting if needed.
- **Risk-Based Approach**: We focus our effort where it matters most to your business.
