## Smart Tax Strategy

Tax compliance is mandatory. Tax strategy is optional — and valuable. We help you meet all Nepal tax requirements while identifying legitimate opportunities to keep more of what you earn. From income tax and VAT to TDS withholding and entity optimization, our tax advisory covers the full spectrum.

### Services Include

- **Income Tax Compliance**: Annual CIT filing, provision calculations, and deduction optimization
- **VAT Management**: Registration, filing, input credit strategies, and reconciliation
- **TDS Withholding**: Payroll and vendor compliance, banking, and quarterly reporting
- **Year-End Planning**: Deduction review, timing strategies, and carry-forward opportunities

### Why Choose Us

- **Proactive, Not Reactive**: We plan before your fiscal year ends, not after.
- **Nepal Tax Expert**: Deep knowledge of IRD rules, recent amendments, and audit practices.
- **Partnership Approach**: Your questions drive our advice, not cookie-cutter templates.
